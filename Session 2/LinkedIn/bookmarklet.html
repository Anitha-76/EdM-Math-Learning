üìù How to Use:

Install the Bookmarklet:
Drag the "Save LinkedIn Job" button above to your browser's bookmarks bar. If you don't see the bookmarks bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac).
Browse LinkedIn Jobs:
Go to LinkedIn and search for jobs as you normally would.
Save a Job:
When you find an interesting job, click the "Save LinkedIn Job" bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar.
Copy the URL:
A popup will appear with the job URL. Copy it (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C).
Add to saved_jobs.txt:
Paste the URL into your saved_jobs.txt file (one URL per line).
Process Jobs:
Run python main.py --process to extract job details and add them to Google Sheets.
